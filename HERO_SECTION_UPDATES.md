### 3. ✅ Body Copy Enhancement

**Before:**
"Delivering peace of mind through comprehensive home evaluations and education. Our meticulous and thorough inspections provide detailed insights to help you make informed decisions about your investment."

**After:**
"James Tubbs, Certified Master Inspector, provides comprehensive home inspections in Cherokee Village, Hardy, and surrounding Arkansas areas. Detailed reports with photos and videos delivered within 24 hours."

### 4. ✅ CTA (Call-to-Action) Hierarchy Reversal

**Before:**
1. Primary CTA: Phone number
2. Secondary CTA: Schedule Inspection

**After:**
1. Primary CTA: Schedule Inspection (blue button)
2. Secondary CTA: Phone number (outline button)

**Additional Enhancements:**
- Phone button now has click-to-call functionality: `tel:5012158700`
- Added urgency indicators below CTAs:
  - "Available This Week" (green checkmark)
  - "Evening & Weekend Appointments" (clock icon)

**Why This Works:**
- Guides users to the booking system (higher conversion)
- Still provides direct phone access
- Urgency indicators reduce friction
- Mobile-friendly click-to-call
